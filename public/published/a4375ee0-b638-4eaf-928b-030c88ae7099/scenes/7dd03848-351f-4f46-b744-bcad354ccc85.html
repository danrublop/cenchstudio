Treatment Approach

🛌Supportive Care
  • Strict rest — avoid exercise for 3–6 months
  • Fluid and sodium restriction
  • Monitor cardiac function closely
  • Treat underlying infection if identified
💊Medications
  • ACE inhibitors / ARBs for heart failure
  • Beta-blockers to control heart rate
  • Diuretics to reduce fluid overload
  • Anti-arrhythmic drugs if needed
  • NSAIDs for pain (with caution)
🧬Immunotherapy
  • Corticosteroids for autoimmune types
  • IVIG in selected cases
  • Immunosuppressants for giant cell type
  • Tailored to biopsy results
🏥Advanced Options
  • Mechanical circulatory support (LVAD)
  • Heart transplant in refractory cases
  • Implantable defibrillator (ICD) if arrhythmia risk
